Hobby

Description Mountain biking is the activity of riding a bicycle offroad, usually with a bike specifically designed to handle the rough and loose conditions that go with riding just about anywhere and everywhere.
My Bike My current mountain bike is a Vitus Sentier VR+ 27.5 hardtail. Vitus makes bikes in Europe and I had it shipped to me to ride. The bike is a size medium with 27.5 inch wheels and has a 435mm reach.
Locations My favourite places to ride include my local woods, along the Credit River, Horseshoe Valley, and Jefferson Forest
